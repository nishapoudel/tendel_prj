About Us

Welcome to Tendel Hardware!

We are a leading supplier of building construction materials, tools, and equipment with multiple branches located in different areas. At Tendel Hardware, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products, exceptional customer service, and competitive prices.

At Tendel Hardware, we believe that exceptional customer service is the key to building long-lasting relationships with our customers. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to answer any questions you may have about our products or services. We can also provide expert advice on which products are best suited for your specific project, ensuring that you have the right tools and materials to get the job done.

At Tendel Hardware, our mission is to provide a comprehensive range of high-quality construction hardware products to meet the diverse needs of our customers. We aim to be the go-to destination for contractors, builders, and do-it-yourself enthusiasts, offering a one-stop solution for all their construction and home improvement requirements.

Our vision is to create a shopping experience that is convenient, efficient, and tailored to the unique needs of our customers. By continuously innovating and staying ahead of industry trends, we strive to be the trusted partner for both professionals and homeowners, providing them with the tools and materials they need to build and improve their spaces.

Our values are at the heart of everything we do. First and foremost, we prioritize quality, offering only top-quality construction hardware products from reputable brands. Customer satisfaction is another key value we hold dear. We listen to our customers' needs, provide personalized assistance, and offer prompt solutions to their queries and concerns.
